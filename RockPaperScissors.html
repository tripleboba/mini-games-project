Rock Paper Scissors Game

How To Play This Game

Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. The rules are very simple and probably you remember them from your childhood: If you choose Rock, you will win against Scissors but lose against Paper. If you choose Scissors, you will win against Paper but lose against Rock. If you choose Paper, you will win against Rock but lose against Scissors. You have 10 rounds. The game ends when rounds over. If you want to play again, just click “Play Again” button after a game is finished. See who wins each round!
